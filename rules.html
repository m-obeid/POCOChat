Before you start using your recently created POCOChat account, you need to follow some rules and conditions. These are about 3 very important topics:

I - What you can and cannot use this service for.
II - How to behave towards other users of this service
III - How collected user information is used

Breaking these rules several times can become an IP-based ban.

This service is offered to you for free by a small developer. This app is not to be abused. Nudity and pornographic content are not allowed. Selling products is also not allowed here. Self-promotion for individuals is allowed as long as it doesn't go too far. Toxic behavior, insulting other users, "doxxing" and other cyber crimes/hyjacks are not allowed. Illegal activities known from the public are of course not welcome here either. Everyone has a free opinion on this platform, and you should always respect what others think.

We want to build a strong, cohesive community. We are nice to each other, we treat others the way we want to be treated.

My app collects very little information. No personal information like your real name or location. Here is the list of everything that is collected:

- IP address to make IP banning possible.
- Your browser user agent, this contains the version and name of the operating system, which web browser you are using and sometimes your device model.
- Device ID and UUID, these are generated only for POCOChat and do not carry any important info.
- Your username and password for POCOChat of course :)

This information is not stored permanently, but serve as a way to search for users in chat, troubleshooting and bug fixing etc....

They are also not shared with any 3rd party but only on the POCOChat database for your account. I will never edit or share this data without your permission.
